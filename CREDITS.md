# Credits

## 3D models

The **vehicles** in `res://models/custom/` — the player **scooter** plus the
**jeepney**, **bus**, **taxi** and **tricycle** traffic — are custom models
generated by the project owner with **Meshy.ai** from original concept images,
then optimised for mobile (textures resized to 1K, mesh simplified, uncompressed
glTF so Godot imports them without a Draco plugin — see `tools/optimize_glb.sh`).
They are the owner's assets.

The **buildings & trees** (`res://models/city/`) are from **Kenney** (kenney.nl),
*Kenney Starter Kit: City Builder* —
<https://github.com/KenneyNL/Starter-Kit-City-Builder> — released under the
**MIT License**, Copyright (c) Kenney (notice retained here as the licence requires).

## Audio

The music loops (`audio/music/`) and sound effects (`audio/sfx/`) are
**procedurally generated** chiptune, synthesised from scratch with a small
Python script (no samples, no third-party audio). They are original to this
project and carry no external licence obligations.

## Everything else

All code, scenes, the scooter/traffic gameplay and UI are original to this
project.
